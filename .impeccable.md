## Design Context

### Users
Concepto serves homeowners, developers, architects, and commercial fit-out teams across London and the South East who need one integrated delivery partner for electrical, smart-home, security, data, and IT systems. They are evaluating credibility quickly and want a site that feels composed, trustworthy, and premium without becoming flashy or vague.

### Brand Personality
Assured, exacting, understated.
The interface should evoke confidence, calm control, and premium craft.

### Aesthetic Direction
Refined luxury with architectural discipline. Warm ivory, stone, smoke, charcoal, and burgundy accents. Editorial display typography for major statements, cleaner modern sans-serif for body copy, and restrained cinematic motion that feels deliberate rather than decorative.

### Design Principles
1. Premium confidence over sales noise.
2. Architectural clarity in layout, spacing, and hierarchy.
3. Restrained theatrical motion with one strong moment per section.
4. Smooth-first implementation that preserves visual richness without dragging local or production performance.
